Angular drills

Part 1: working in the view

   The aim of "Angular drills part 1" is to focus on the view by using native angularJS directives, to play with UI states and to resist the temptation to use controllers or directives.
   During part one, all drills have to be performed without the use of any controller or custom directive.

Drill 1.3 Nested ng-repeat with filters

Using two ng-repeat directives (nested), display a 2 levels list with continents at the top level and countries at the bottom level.
Countries need to be correctly listed below the continent they are in. Countries are available in the scope (variable `countries`, see in ng-init directive).
  • Ng-click directive: http://docs.angularjs.org/api/ng.directive:ngClick
  • Ng-repeat directive: http://docs.angularjs.org/api/ng.directive:ngRepeat
  • Filters: http://docs.angularjs.org/api/ng.filter:filter
